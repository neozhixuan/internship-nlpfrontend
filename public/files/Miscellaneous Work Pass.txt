Who it is for	Eligible foreigners on short-term assignments, such as speakers at a seminar, religious workers or journalists.
Who can apply	An employer or local sponsor needs to apply for the pass.
What is the duration	Up to 60 days.
Is it renewable	No.
Passes for family	Not available.
Foreign worker levy or quota	No foreign worker levy or quota required.
Relevant legislation	Conditions of Work Pass
Employment of Foreign Manpower Act
